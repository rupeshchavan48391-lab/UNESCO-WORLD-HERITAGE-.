About UNESCO

The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) was founded in 1945 to promote peace and security through international cooperation in education, science, culture, and communication. UNESCO, the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization, is a UN specialized agency dedicated to promoting peace and security through international cooperation in education, the sciences, culture, and communication. It sets global standards, develops international cooperation tools, produces knowledge for public policies, and builds networks of World Heritage Sites and institutions to address global challenges and foster equality. a It designates World Heritage Sites of outstanding universal value.
